How to Access Your Environment

  • Request the slip of paper from your cohort lead with your GUID on it.
  • Download your environment's private key as instructed on the paper.
  • Use the private key to SSH into your bastion host
    • ssh -i <private key> ec2-user@bastion.${GUID}.example.opentlc.com
